// KQL Query: Detect Brute Force Login Attempts
// Author: Ahmad Fahim Naweed
// Description: Identifies accounts with 10+ failed login attempts within a 5-minute window.

SecurityEvent
| where EventID == 4625
| summarize FailedAttempts = count() by Account, Computer, bin(TimeGenerated, 5m)
| where FailedAttempts >= 10
| sort by FailedAttempts desc
